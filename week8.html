One advantage of a time-frequency analysis over a waveform-based analysis is that time frequency analysis proves a more comprehensive analysis of a piece. A time-frequency analysis provides shows sound through a graph, where the x-axis represents time, the y-axis represents frequency and colour represents amplitude. Whereas a waveform-based analysis shows sound through a graph where the x-axis represents time and the y-axis represents amplitude. While the waveform is beneficial for editing audio as it provides a clearer visualisation of the volume and duration of sound, time frequency is more useful analysis as it provides more information.
