Week 2 🎜 Music as Notation

🎜 Identify a piece of music related to your theme to download and edit.
🎜 If you are using your own machine, you can use the Audiveris OMR tool to get started, or you can transcribe it from scratch.
🎜 Edit at least 10 bars in MuseScore, focusing on rhythm and pitch as a minimum.

I transcribed seasons by wave to earth from scratch, then made some edits. I changed the tempo from 72 beats per minute to 80 beats per minute. I also added in dynamics, 'mf' - mezzo forte meaning moderatly loud. Furthermore, I changed the pitch of some notes throughout the peice.
