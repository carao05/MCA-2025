Week 5 🎝 Standards in Curation

Task 1

🎝 Create a metadata schema of at least 5 elements, including title, artist, publisher, and at least two others of your choice.

Title= name of transcribed score
Composer = name of person that wrote the music
Publisher = name of company that published the work
Lyricist = name of person that wrote the lyrics to the song
Encoder = name of person that encoded the score
Encoding Date = date of encoding

Task 2

🎝 Modify your MEI document according to the schema that you have created.
